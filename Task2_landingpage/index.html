VARIETIES WE SERVE

Apple

This red, medium-sized fruit becomes fully ripe in mid-October. we offer a range of varieties from sweet and juicy Fuji to tart and tangy Granny Smith, ensuring the perfect apple for every taste preference.

Orange

we offer a delightful selection of oranges, including the sweet and juicy navel oranges, zesty and tangy Valencia oranges perfect for juicing, and the vibrant blood oranges with their unique flavor and stunning color.

Grapes

our grape selection features a range of delectable varieties to suit every taste. Enjoy the crisp sweetness of red grapes like Crimson and Flame, or savor the refreshing tartness of green grapes.

Dragon fruit

we're excited to offer the exotic and striking dragon fruit. With its vibrant pink or yellow skin and speckled flesh,dragon fruit are delights with a subtly sweet, mildly tangy flavor.

Kiwi

our selection of kiwi offers a delightful burst of tropical flavor and nutrition. Known for its fuzzy brown skin and vibrant green flesh speckled with tiny black seeds, kiwi provides a perfect balance of sweetness and tang

Pomegranate

our pomegranates are a true jewel of the fruit world, offering a stunning display of ruby-red seeds packed with juicy sweetness and a hint of tartness.
